About me
Today I wish you what is good and only.
The bad thing about being myself is that, as negative and realistic as I seem to be on the outside, deep down I'll always have that boring hope until the last minute of the second half. I'm always going to want to believe in the good side of people until proven otherwise. Sometimes, even after it proves otherwise. I hide from the world this unshaken faith of mine because when reality appears and things are not as they should be, it destroys me. It frustrates me. It's like they've been sohigh a thousand times in my stomach. My tireless waiting always tires me out. And no one needs to know that. It's like I'm telling the person on my side, "it's not going to work, I don't care, never mind," while I mentally repeat, "please, i'm wrong.
You'll always be mine..
- January, 1999.
